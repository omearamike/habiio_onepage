Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We respect
you and your privacy while using our website
or mobile applications.

App users
The habits you add to Habi (the app) are private
and visible to only to you. We don’t ask you for
personal information, nor do we want it.

Statistics
You can use Habi (the app) to store statistics
about your Habit building progress. This
information is private and visible to only to you,
unless you choose to post your progress to social
networks from the application.

Website visitors
Habi may collect information like Internet
Protocol (IP) addresses from users visiting Habi.io

Cookies
Habi reserves the right to use Cookies.
A cookie is message given to a web browser by
a web server. The browser stores the message in
a text file. The message is then sent back to
the server each time the browser requests a
page from the server. Habi visitors who do not
wish to have cookies placed on their computers
should set their browsers to refuse cookies
before using Habi's websites.

Privacy policy changes
Although most changes are likely to be minor,
Habi may change its Privacy Policy from time
to time, and in Habi's sole discretion. Habi
encourages visitors to frequently check this
privacy page for any changes to its Privacy
Policy. Your continued use of this site after
any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute
your acceptance of such change.
